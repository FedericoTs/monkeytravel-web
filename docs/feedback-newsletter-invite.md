# Newsletter invite — "help shape MonkeyTravel" (feedback survey)

Drop-in copy for a newsletter section inviting active users to log back in and
take the in-app feedback survey. Pairs with the in-app survey (auto-shown on
/trips to users with ≥1 trip) and the personal outreach email
(`lib/email/templates/FeedbackOutreach.tsx`).

**Rules**
- Sign-off is **"The MonkeyTravel Team"** — never a personal name.
- No mention of price/payment — the product is positioned as free.
- Link target: `https://monkeytravel.app/trips` (the survey appears there for
  active users). Power users you email personally get a direct tokenized
  `/feedback/<token>` link instead — see the outreach email.

---

## English

**Heading:** We'd love your take

You've actually used MonkeyTravel to plan real trips — which makes your opinion
worth more than a hundred from people who never have. We're deciding what to
build next, and 30 seconds of honest feedback would genuinely shape it.

Log back in and you'll see a short survey on your trips page. No right answers,
no fluff — just tell us what you use it for and what almost made you quit.

**Button:** Open MonkeyTravel → https://monkeytravel.app/trips

— The MonkeyTravel Team
