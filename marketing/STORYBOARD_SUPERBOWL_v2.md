# "Feed the Monkey" — Super Bowl spots (30–60s) · VALIDATE FIRST

No images/video generated from this until you approve it.

## The engine (ties the gag to a REAL feature)
The MonkeyTravel logo-monkey is a living cartoon **gremlin companion** riding with
the explorer. In the app you earn **🍌 bananas by inviting friends** (real
mechanic: "referrals → Explorer / Invite Friends"). Bananas are the monkey's fuel:
- **Fed** (you invited friends) → monkey is calm, blissful, helpful.
- **Starving** (you went solo) → monkey goes **feral**: tricks the explorer, tosses
  him across the world, sabotages — absurd slapstick.
Our explorer is a proud loner → perpetually out of bananas → perpetually getting
tossed. Resolution every spot: he taps **Invite Friends** → 🍌 rain → monkey calm.

**Tagline / end line:** "Feed the monkey. Invite your friends." + *"Friends sold separately."*

## Tone
Extremely ironic, deadpan, absurd. The explorer narrates like a calm nature-doc host
*while* being physically abused by a small monkey. He never breaks. Super Bowl polish.

## Transition toolkit (every cut is a designed, seamless transition)
- **Banana-whip:** monkey hurls a banana past lens → whip-pan into the next location.
- **Screen-portal:** phone screen fills frame → push *into* it → emerge in next scene.
- **Toss match-action:** monkey throws the explorer out frame-left → he flies in
  frame-right in the next place.
- **Object match-cut:** round banana ↔ sun ↔ plate ↔ porthole ↔ disco light.
- **Hat-wipe:** explorer's hat swipes the lens → reveal.
- **Monkey-leap:** monkey jumps across the cut, landing in the new shot.
Design note: shoot each shot with a clean entry/exit on one side so cuts feel continuous.
