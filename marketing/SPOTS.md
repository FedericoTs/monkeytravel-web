dance — stiff, ritualistic, *deadly serious* — in a packed club while everyone
around him parties normally. He is the most out-of-place man alive and could not
care less.
**Joke:** his trip's already sorted, so he has nothing to do but dance.
**Line (pick one):**
- "When you plan a whole trip in 30 seconds… you've got time to kill."

**Screen beat:** 1s cut to phone — itinerary **"Plan ready · 30s."**
**End:** logo + *"Plan in 30s. Free your nights."*
**Why it pops:** meme format + total deadpan contrast = instant share + sound-driven.

## SPOT 3 — "No Bananas" · invite friends → bananas → calm monkey · ~15s
**Scene:** The explorer is being **dragged by one foot** across the forest floor,
face-down through mud and leaves, by the tiny logo-monkey — while calmly narrating
to camera as if everything's fine.
**Joke:** he forgot the one rule, so the monkey's gone feral.
**Line (deadpan, mid-drag):**
- "Invite friends, you earn bananas. Bananas keep him calm. I have… zero bananas."
- "This is what 'traveling alone' looks like."
**Screen beat:** the phone in his dragging hand — **Invite Friends · +50 🍌** (balance: 0).
**End:** monkey, now calm and holding a banana 🍌 + logo + *"Invite friends. Earn bananas. Keep him calm."*
**Why it pops:** physical comedy + serene narration + the gremlin = very shareable, and it sells the referral mechanic.

## SPOT 4 — "Democracy" · group voting · ~15s
**Scene:** The explorer sits in the middle of an absurdly elaborate jungle
**glamping** camp — chandeliers in the trees, a velvet sofa, fairy lights —
surrounded by a big, warm crowd of welcoming locals/new friends, everyone holding
phones. He turns to camera, slightly overwhelmed.
**Joke:** he let "the group" vote on activities… the whole village outvoted him,
and glamping escalated wildly.
**Line:**
- "I let the group vote on the activities. I didn't expect glamping to get this… wild."
- "We all voted. They chose chaos. (And a chandelier.)"
**Screen beat:** voting screen — activities with big vote tallies, **"Glamping"** winning in a landslide.
**End:** logo + *"Everyone votes. (You've been warned.)"*
**⚠️ Execution note:** depict the crowd as a **warm, modern, respectful community of
new friends / fellow travelers** — NOT a costume/stereotype caricature of an
Indigenous "tribe." Keep the joke on the *scale of the group* and the over-the-top
glamping, not on any culture. (Tell me if you'd rather it be e.g. a wedding party,
a hostel crew, or a festival crowd.)

## SPOT 5 — "Concrete Jungle" · in-app AI concierge · ~15s

**Line:**

- *(concierge replies)* "…civilization. Twelve meters east. I'll make it."
**Screen beat:** AI concierge chat — he asks, it answers with a rec + directions.
**End:** logo + *"Your AI concierge. Anywhere."*
