# Original viral concepts (no memes) — brainstorm

Deadpan-absurd, unexpected, ownable. Same explorer (Soul→relight) + the logo
monkey gremlin + the banana economy (invite friends → 🍌 → calm monkey).
Tagline bank: "Friends sold separately." · "Our AI runs on bananas."

## ⭐ Top picks (most ownable + viral)

**A. "The AI is just the monkey" (reveal)**
Cut behind the scenes at MonkeyTravel HQ: the "world-class AI" planning your trip
is literally the logo monkey, furiously typing at a tiny desk buried in banana
peels. An engineer lobs in a banana → monkey perks up → your itinerary instantly
gets better. *"Our AI runs on bananas. Invite friends, feed the monkey."*
Why: absurd reveal, 100% ownable, secretly explains the referral mechanic.

**B. "Hostage Negotiation"**
Explorer across a tiny table from the monkey, ONE banana between them, deadpan
stand-off. *"You let me leave the hotel… the banana is yours."* The monkey slides
a fresh itinerary across the table. He is not in charge. The monkey is.
Why: power-flip, two-hander, freeze-frames are instantly meme-able.

**C. "Party of One"**
App says "plan with up to 8 friends." He takes it literally — shows up with 8
passports, a table for 8, toasts all 8 empty glasses himself, does a one-man
Mexican wave. *"Up to 8 friends. (Friends sold separately.)"*
Why: literal-minded gag + relatable loneliness; cheap to shoot.

## More angles

**D. "Nothing Left to Survive"** — Hardcore survivalist (face paint, machete)
gets dropped by the app into a *flawless all-inclusive resort.* Tries to start a
fire by the pool bar; "rations water" that's a piña colada; no one to fight.
*"I trained for the wild. It booked me a swim-up suite."*

**E. "Speedrun: Rome any%"** — Treats the chill, perfectly-routed itinerary like a
video-game speedrun: stopwatch, on-screen splits, sprinting between optimized
stops. *"New personal best."* Because the walking routes are *too* good.

**F. "The Jealous Map"** — A dusty fold-out paper map watches, devastated, as he
plans everything on the app and tosses it in a drawer. One slow tear. *"Retire the map."*

**G. "He Made It Hard on Purpose"** — App gives the easy route; his ego refuses. He
machetes through hedges, scales a wall, swims a canal… and arrives at the exact
café the app pinned, soaked, 40 min late. Monkey waiting, tapping its wrist.
*"Or… just follow the plan."*

**H. "Still Deciding"** — On screen: a group chat "planning Lisbon" since 2019 —
247 unread, zero decisions. Cut to the explorer: opens app, 30 seconds, done,
already on the plane. *"Some groups are still deciding."*

## Recommendation
Lead with **A** (monkey-is-the-AI) or **B** (hostage negotiation) — both are
uniquely ours, hilarious, and teach the bananas/referral hook without feeling
like an ad. **C** is the cheapest punchy backup.
